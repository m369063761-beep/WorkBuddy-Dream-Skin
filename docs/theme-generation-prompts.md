# Theme background generation prompts

These prompts target a wide WorkBuddy window. Keep characters on the right and reserve the left sidebar plus lower-center composer as low-detail UI-safe zones.

Exact franchise character images are not bundled in the public repository. Confirm that you have the right to use and redistribute any image you install.

## Luffy Gear 5

```text
Create a polished cinematic anime desktop wallpaper featuring Monkey D. Luffy in Gear 5 / Nika form: cloud-white flame-like hair, white open robe and shorts, purple sash, white cloud scarf, joyful fearless grin, dynamic rubbery pose, subtle violet-black lightning. Moonlit open ocean, airy white clouds, pale moon glow and restrained warm gold. Wide 16:9 composition; Luffy in the rightmost 30%, facing inward. Keep the left 28% and central lower half calm, dark and low-detail for application UI. No UI, text, logo, watermark, border or extra characters.
```
